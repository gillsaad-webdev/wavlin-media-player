About Artist

Shubh is one of the most exciting and talented Punjabi rappers and singers to emerge in recent years. Born as Shubhneet Singh in Punjab, India, he later moved to Canada where he developed his unique musical style. Growing up between two cultures, Shubh beautifully blends traditional Punjabi emotions with modern hip-hop, R&B, and trap beats, creating a sound that feels both fresh and deeply rooted in his heritage.
He first gained massive attention with his breakout hit "Moves", which went viral across India , Pakistan, Canada, and other world Wide . The song showcased his smooth flow, catchy hooks, and effortless ability to connect with listeners.
Following that success, tracks like "Broken" , "Supreme", and many others further cemented his place in the Punjabi music industry.
What makes Shubh truly special is his authenticity. He writes music that reflects real emotions — heartbreak, ambition, street life, and self-belief. His deep voice combined with powerful lyrics gives his songs a raw and honest feel that millions of fans relate to deeply. With tens of millions of streams on Spotify and a massive growing fanbase across the globe, Shubh is not just a trend — he is a movement.
He represents the new generation of Punjabi artists who are taking their culture to the world stage with confidence and pride!
